How to get to the School of International relations

The School of International Relations is located in the heart of the historic city centre, in a building belonging to the ensemble of the Smolny Monastery. You should enter the building from the Rastrelli square.

There is a good transport connection to the the School of International Relations:

  • from the metro station " Chernyshevskaya ": buses 22, 46, 105, 136; taxi buses 15 , 46, 51, 76, 90, 105K, 136, 163, 167, 269;
  • from the metro station " Ploshchad Vosstaniya - Mayakovskaya ": buses 22, 54, 74, 105, trolley buses 5, 7, 11;
  • if you would like to use taxi, we recommend you to install the Gett app (дать ссылку) http://gett.com/
